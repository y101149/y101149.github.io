I was born!!!

On 19th July 1991, I was born in Jining Inner Mongolia. The day I was born, the very forest of the world whispered my name ...Yixin Yin. I went to Gongnonglu primary school in September 1998 and I have a great childhood there.

19th July 1991

Go to Beijing

On 13th July 2001, the day Beijing won the bid for The 2008 Olympic Games, I left my hometown and went to Beijing with my parents. I went to Zhongshanjie primary school in Tongzhou District, Beijing. Then in September 2004, I went to No.4 Middle School of Tontzhou District.

13th July 2001

Go to High School

In September 2007,I went to High School attached to Tsinghua University. From that day, I started to live at school. I got a lot of freedom and I enjoyed that. During my high school life, I spent a lot of time playing basketball and I met a lot of friends by playing basketball. It was a amazing time in my life. Finally I got 629 points in the College Entrance Exam.

September 2007

Go to College

In September 2010, I went to Beihang University and my major is computer science. From that day, I began to learn how to code. Before I enter college, I had never touched programming. I even don't know what C language is. So at the very beginning of Grade 1, As a fresh man, my life is hard. I need to learn a lot of courses I never heard before. It is almost a disaster for me. Luckily I spent some time on learning and it worked. I joined the Software and Engineering Lab of Beihang University in September 2013. I also finished my graduation project there. The topic of my work is The Magma Log Management System. I used hibernate + struts2 + jsp + mysql database to finish the system. Luckily I graduate successfully in June 2014 and get my bachelor degree. I learnt a lot of knowledge related to computer science during the four years. But I know it is insufficient, what I need to learn is more in the future.

September 2010

Go Abroad

On 15th August 2014, I arrived Washington DC and started a new life there. I went to George Washington University in order to get my Master Degree. My major is still computer science there. Living in another country is really amazing for me. I felt the culture shock when I came here at first. But everything goes right now. I learnt more on how to live in the world rather than simple knowledge. Hope everything goes in the right way.

15th August 2014

Practice in Wecash

Last summer I joined Wecash company as an intern. I attended Nodejs Web Application Department. I learnt a lot of knowledge on Nodejs there. I also wrote some interface to practice my programming ability. That was really cool. The company is basically working on loan and credit.The experience there was great for me. I coded everyday and played basketball on every Thursday. I hope I could have opportunity to work there in the future.

Now I am planning to go back to China for better development because I think I feel more comfortable with the atmosphere of China. Furthermore my family is in Beijing and I love my parents. So I enjoy the time with them. Hope everything goes right.
